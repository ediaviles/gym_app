HealthWeb

HealthWeb is a website meant for people who are interested in gym and health related subjects. We provide tools for logging and visualizing your progress as well as a way of meeting other users who share similar interests as you!

Tools

Daily Diary

A diary used to keep track of different variables such as macronutrients, weight, and weight lifted at the gym. These variables can be graphed in the progress section of the website.

Progression

Visualize your progress in a dynamic and engaing way. Graphs are updated accordingly to your daily diary submissions.
